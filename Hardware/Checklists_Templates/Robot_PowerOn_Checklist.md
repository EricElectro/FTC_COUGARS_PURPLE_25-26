# Robot Power-On Checklist

This checklist is intended to guide you through the necessary steps to safely power on the robot. Please follow each step carefully to ensure proper operation.

## Power-On Steps

1. Ensure that the robot is on a flat, stable surface.
2. Check all connections to the control system and ensure they are secure.
3. Verify that the battery is fully charged and properly connected.
4. Power on the control system and wait for it to initialize.
5. Confirm that all motors and sensors are functioning correctly.
6. Perform a system check to ensure all components are operational.
7. If any issues are detected, troubleshoot and resolve them before proceeding.
8. Once all checks are complete, the robot is ready for operation.

> Add or modify steps as necessary for your specific robot configuration.
